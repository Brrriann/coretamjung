# Detective Agency Website Project Blueprint (v2.1 - Visual Overhaul)

## Overview
A high-trust, conversion-optimized website for "Core Private Investigation (Core Detective)". 
Version 2.1 introduces a cinematic visual experience in the hero section.

## Terminology Strategy (Ad Compliance)
- **Maintain:** Professional, legal, and trust-focused terms ( 사실 관계 규명, 부정행위 사실 확인, 정황 증거 수집).

## Design & Aesthetic (Cinematic Trust)
- **Hero Section:** Full-screen background video (Dark, professional, moody) with an overlay.
- **Animations:** "Heavy" fade-in for the main slogan (Blur -> Clear, Fade-in, Subtle Slide-up).
- **Color Palette:** Royal Navy, Gold, and White (High contrast over video).

## Core Sections & Features
1. **Visual Hero Section (NEW):** Auto-playing background video + Cinematic slogan animation.
2. **Pain Point Section:** Identify with the client's emotional distress.
3. **Core Services:** Re-branded with professional terms.
4. **Differentiation:** 24/7 Secret HQ, Legal Counsel verification.
5. **Process Timeline:** Transparent 4-step investigation workflow.
6. **Safe-Consultation:** Privacy-first contact form.

## Implementation Steps
1. **Update `index.html`:** Replace Hero content with video container and updated slogan structure.
2. **Update `style.css`:** Implement video background styles, overlays, and "heavy" keyframe animations.
3. **Update `main.js`:** Ensure video handles correctly and trigger animations.
4. **Push to GitHub:** Document versions.
